Welcome to BlockChase Maze Adventure

In this game, you will navigate through mazes, pushing boxes to targets while avoiding various monsters.

Monsters

Horny

An orange demon with menacing horns. This relentless fiend constantly hunts the player, moving directly towards them. When obstructed, Horny resorts to random movement. Its speed is notably brisk, adding a sense of urgency to the chase.

Ghost

A spectral entity donned in a red hat. After the player takes 6 steps, Ghost spawns at the player’s starting point and mimics their movements, trailing their exact path. Despite its spectral nature, Ghost moves at a sluggish pace, creating a creeping tension.

Sleepy

A dual-natured red monster with a black hat. Sleepy alternates between two phases every 5 seconds: a dormant phase where it remains completely still, and an active phase where it dashes towards the player with remarkable speed. It never moves randomly, maintaining a focused pursuit even with obstacles in the way.

Alien

A peculiar yellow creature with three eyes. Alien generally moves swiftly in a straight path but unpredictably changes direction upon hitting obstacles or entering new 6x6 grid zones. Its erratic movement adds an element of surprise and challenge.

Body

A large, slow-moving creature that occupies a 2x2 space. It moves randomly and starts at one of the four central intersection points of the game area.

Plague

A green, slimy mass with terrifying teeth at its center. This monster starts small but grows as it moves, leaving a trail behind. It can grow up to five segments long, creating a challenging obstacle for the player to navigate around.
